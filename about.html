About

My Story

I am a UX/UI designer based in London. I have a background in creative design and sales. I have experience working as a design freelancer, where I communicated with clients to understand their needs, working together to create an end product they loved. I also have experience working in sales, where I acquired various selling techniques. UX Design to me was sort of a ‘aha moment’ as I have a unique design background and a creative problem-solving approach but I couldn’t find the perfect path for me to go down, until UX design. I want to use my sales experience and love of digital innovation to create meaningful products that can positively impact people's lives.
